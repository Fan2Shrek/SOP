| HALT | 0111 |  /   |   /   |   /   |    /    |
| JMP  | 1000 |  a   |   /   |   /   |    /    |
| JEQZ | 1001 |  a   |   b   |   c   |    /    |
| JEQ  | 1010 |  a   |   b   |   c   |    /    |
